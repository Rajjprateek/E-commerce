Our Vision

Wardrobe Angel works by spreading awareness about slow and responsible fashion, along with recycling, reusing and upcycling of clothes. We collect donations from people all over India, with a current focus on Delhi. All appropriate clothing is sorted and sent to those in need through a broad network of partner NGOs. High-end pieces and designer items that come in as donations from India's film and fashiom influencers are used at Wardrobe Angel Annual Garage Sale and Wardrobe Angel Pop-up events to raise awareness for second - hand clothing and, of course, to raise funds for our outreach project and operations.
We truly believe that pre-loved clothing and responsible practices are the way into the future of fashion and hope that our events lead Delhi to become a hub for thrift stores in India.


At Wardrobe Angel, we believe that Fashion can empower people. With your contribution, we can clothe more than a hundred thousand people across india every year.

Your Donations are segregated into three categories :
