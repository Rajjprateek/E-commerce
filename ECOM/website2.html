WARDROBE ANGEL

Here's how to donate your clothes in 4 easy steps :



CLEAR

Clear your closet. Take out all the clothes you haven't worn in over 6 months, unless they are seasonal, you sure won't wear them again!

WASH

At Wardrobe Angel, we appreciate quality clothes, freshly washed and nicely folded to make things easier for us to segregate.

PACK

Simply put all the clothes in a box and send it over to us. It would be great if you could add a letter to your package stating the number of items you have donated to us.

CALL

Get in touch with our Wardrobe Angel team at info@wardrobeangel.com or give us a call on +91-9818899778 to schedule your donation pick-up.

LET'S CONNECT

If you resonate with our idea of the Wardrobe Angel project, do send us an email to :
info@wardrobeangel.com
